**Project: Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis**
This is an industry-ready, end-to-end data analytics project that transforms 3,900 raw transactional records into actionable business intelligence.  OverviewThe project follows a complete data lifecycle to uncover insights into spending patterns, product preferences, and subscription behavior:  Data Pipeline: Data loading and cleaning using pandas (standardizing to snake case, removing redundant columns).  
**Infrastructure: **Cleaned data is loaded into PostgreSQL for rigorous SQL analysis.  Visualization: An interactive Power BI dashboard tracks KPIs including revenue, avg. purchase amount, and subscription status.   
Key InsightsRevenue Drivers: Male customers generate over 2x the revenue of females.  Demographics: Young Adults are the top revenue-contributing age group.  Subscription Gap: While non-subscribers drive 73% of total revenue due to volume, they represent a major opportunity for conversion.  Discount Sensitivity: 839 high-spending customers utilize discounts even when spending above the average amount ($59.76), signaling a need for margin control. 
**Strategic RecommendationsConvert Non-Subscribers:** Promote exclusive benefits to capture the 73% non-subscriber majority.  Enhance Loyalty: Implement reward programs to shift repeat buyers into the "Loyal" segment.  Refine Discounts: Re-evaluate discount policies to protect margins on high-value transactions.  Targeted Marketing: Align marketing campaigns with high-revenue age groups and express-shipping users.
