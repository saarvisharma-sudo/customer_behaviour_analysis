# **Project: Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis**
This is an industry-ready, end-to-end data analytics project that transforms 3,900 raw transactional records into actionable business intelligence.  OverviewThe project follows a complete data lifecycle to uncover insights into spending patterns, product preferences, and subscription behavior:  Data Pipeline: Data loading and cleaning using pandas (standardizing to snake case, removing redundant columns).  

## **Infrastructure:** 
Cleaned data is loaded into PostgreSQL for rigorous SQL analysis.  Visualization: An interactive Power BI dashboard tracks KPIs including revenue, avg. purchase amount, and subscription status.   

## **Key InsightsRevenue Drivers:**
Male customers generate over 2x the revenue of females.  Demographics: Young Adults are the top revenue-contributing age group.  Subscription Gap: While non-subscribers drive 73% of total revenue due to volume, they represent a major opportunity for conversion.  Discount Sensitivity: 839 high-spending customers utilize discounts even when spending above the average amount ($59.76), signaling a need for margin control. 

## **Strategic RecommendationsConvert Non-Subscribers:**
Promote exclusive benefits to capture the 73% non-subscriber majority.  Enhance Loyalty: Implement reward programs to shift repeat buyers into the "Loyal" segment.  Refine Discounts: Re-evaluate discount policies to protect margins on high-value transactions. ## **Technical Pipeline**
The project follows a structured methodology for robust, reproducible analysis:Data Loading & Cleaning:Utilized pandas for initial data exploration and preprocessing.  Standardized column naming to snake_case.  Cleaned data, including dropping redundant features (e.g., promo_code_used).  Database Integration:Cleaned datasets were loaded into PostgreSQL for scalable, complex SQL analysis.  Visualization & Insights:Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to provide real-time visibility into key performance indicators (KPIs) such as average purchase amount, subscription rates, and revenue distribution. 

##**Key FindingsRevenue Drivers:**
Male customers generated more than 2x the revenue of female customers.  Age Engagement: Young Adults represent the highest revenue-contributing age group.  Subscription Gap: While average spend is similar between groups, non-subscribers drive 73% of total revenue due to higher volume; this presents a significant conversion opportunity.  Discount Strategy: 839 high-spending customers utilized discounts despite spending above the average purchase amount ($59.76), indicating a need for refined margin control. 

## **Strategic Recommendations**
Based on the analysis, the following actions are recommended to drive business growth:Boost Subscriptions: Promote exclusive benefits to convert the 73% non-subscriber majority.  Loyalty Programs: Implement targeted rewards for repeat buyers to transition them into the "Loyal" segment.  Optimize Discount Policy: Review current discount structures to ensure they do not erode margins for high-spenders.  Targeted Marketing: Focus marketing efforts on top-performing age groups and high-value product categories.
